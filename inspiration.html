Beaches & Coastline

Sefton's coast has so much to offer residents and visitors. Miles of beaches and sand dunes to explore, woodland and estuary which play host to an amazing amount of wildlife and some of the most rare plants and animals in the country, all promise an almost infinite universe of discovery! Delve further in to the shipwrecks and prehistoric footprints which lay trapped for thousands of years in sediment beds and Antony Gormley’s “Another Place” installation on Crosby beach which offers the biggest open air art gallery in the country.

Shopping

Exciting, colourful and cultured, Albert Dock is Liverpool’s historic heart. A year-round events and exhibitions programme animates the area's iconic cast iron columns. Independent shops burst with crafts, photography and art. Day or night, summer or winter, visit for a day or stay for a week.There so many places that you can do shopping over here!

Shiraz is a great choice for a date, a meal out, a party or celebration. We are passionate about good food, good service and creating a warm, friendly atmosphere for our guests.

6 / 6
Beaches & Coastlines

Beaches & Coastlines

Formby Beach is just one of Merseyside's stunning coastal gems. This particular coastal area is undergoing erosion and the tides often reveal prehistoric mud layers. Look closely and you might just see some human and animal footprints! The high sand dunes not only offer fantastic fun, but amazing views across the Irish Sea and on a really clear day, the mountains of Cumbria can be seen. There's also picnic areas, waymarked paths to the beach and woods to explore.
